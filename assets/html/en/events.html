Œuvres Italiennes au XVIIIème

January 14, 2024 – Sainte-Colombe church, Chevilly-Larue

Messe Basse Production is proud and honored to organize another exceptionnal concert starring Guillaume Beaulieu and Julien Faure. Concert « Un voyage musical Baroque »

December 9, 2023 – Centre Isadora Duncan, Igny

In order to complete a busy year for La Baroquerie du Val de Bièvre, they offered one last concert in Igny's centre Isadora Duncan, to give a last round for pieces worked along the year. Concert Estival

July 2, 2023 – Saint-Côme & Saint-Damien church, Villebon-sur-Yvette

To celebrate the rising summer, La Baroquerie du Val de Bièvre gave a concert that displayed the wide variety of the baroque era, with concertos and suites from Telemann, Holborne's dances, extracts of an Albinoni's concerto and a rare and exclusive piece from polish composer Gorczycki.
